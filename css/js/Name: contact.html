🚨 Victims of Cryptocurrency Fraud

If you have lost funds to a scam, hack, or fraudulent scheme, please contact us immediately. Time is critical for asset freezing.

Email:

victims@cipherrescuechains.com

What to include:

  • All transaction hashes (TXIDs)
  • Wallet addresses involved (yours and the scammer's)
  • Any communication with the scammer (screenshots, emails, messages)
  • Police report number (if filed)
  • Estimated loss amount

⚠️ Important: We take confidentiality seriously. All information is protected under strict privacy protocols. Do not share private keys or seed phrases with anyone.
